How it Works

Requesting your Order is very simple:
  • Just open the app and Pick where you’re going.
  • The app uses your location so your driver knows where to pick you up.
  • You’ll see your driver picture, details, and can track their arrival on the map
  • If you have no place to stay you can choose from the various accommodation and options we offer within a click.
  • Payment for the transportation services can be made by credit card, Android Pay, PayPal and cash in selected cities.
  • Payment for accommodations requires a valid credit or debit card.
  • After the ride / the stay, you can rate your driver / property and provide feedback to help us improve In a click experience.

How Can I book a accommodation

All accommodations can be booked immediately within a click, with a valid credit card and you will receive a confirmation email and phone SMS (If you do not insert a valid email address and mobile number you will not receive the confirmation details ). In some cases an inquiry must be sent first. In these instances the accommodation is not booked until the inquiry is confirmed by the accommodation provider.

Apartments, Rooms, Villas ... etc Will apper on the map according to your Choise:

How can I book a ride with in a click?

1- Place your Request
Enter your pickup location and your destination address (your default pickup point is set to your current GPS location). Choose your preferred car type , get a fare estimate , and tap request. then your driver will arrive in minutes.

2-Ride
Your ride comes to you. You will see your driver's contact information and vehicle details in the app, so you know you are getting in the right car.

3-Pay
Pay with credit card on file or cash.

4- Rate your experience
Rate your driver when you reach you destination.
